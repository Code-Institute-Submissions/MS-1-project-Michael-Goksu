Our Vision...

We've always loved gyming and gaming so we decided to create a haven for such liking

We love gyming, we love gaming, and for you to do both shouldn't really occupy half the day, so we decided to kill the traveling time for those who hate loosing time on traveling, and added a food service on the spot, we are a team of 20people, all coming from different background but all who love gyming and gaming, or as we call it GnG time! For you, our customer, we provide a gym with regular cleaning of the facility, personal training, gym classes/sessions supervised by the great Arnold himself! and for our gaming area: Our vision here is to help you relax, play, enjoy, compete, we throw occasional events/tournaments(price pool differs, depends on sponsor and how well we do!) we have rules for how one should behave within these halls. Failure to apply results in warning, after 3 you're out for good. At the end of the day, everyone should be able to come here, feel like home and just relax or just do their thing, do their passion, get your GnG time going! We look forward to growing, and we are thankful to all our customers!
